Core services

Solutions to high-stakes problems

From the first line of a tender document to the resolution of a complex dispute, we provide specialist advisory across every stage of the contract and project lifecycle — protecting your interests and advancing your outcomes. We support both clients and contractors across Egypt, KSA, and the wider MENA region.
Contracts Management
Drafting, review, and full administration for both clients and contractors. Bespoke contractual architecture covering FIDIC, NEC, and JCT suites tailored to local civil codes — governing the complete project lifecycle.
Pre & Post Contract
Claims Management
Forensic delay analysis, quantum analysis for cost claims, claim preparation, submission, and defense. Strategic recovery for contractors and strategic defense for employers — built on precision and precedent.
EOT & Cost Claims
Project Planning & Control
CPM scheduling, baseline development, delay analysis using Impacted As-Planned and Time Impact Analysis methods. Progress audits and cash-flow preparation — integrating the schedule with the law.
Scheduling & Delay
Risk Management
Qualitative and quantitative risk assessment, risk registers, and mitigation strategies. Scenario modelling and contingency planning tailored to the geopolitical and supply-chain complexities of the MENA region.
Risk & Mitigation
Dispute Management
Written submissions and representation in DB, DAB, and DRB proceedings. Full support for arbitration including counsel attendance, expert witness statements, and claim consultancy for both parties.
Arbitration & DAB
Procurement Strategy
Tender documentation preparation, evaluation, and award advice. Procurement approaches aligned with applicable guidelines, regulatory compliance, RFP preparation, and supply chain management.
